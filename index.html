How to Use:

1.) Using the dropdown menu below, select your library and then press the "Go" button. If you are accessing from a listed library, then skip steps below and click the "In-Library Use" button

2.) A new browser tab will open and you will be prompted to enter your library barcode number to sign in to the ProQuest database

3.) After signing in to the database, close that browser tab and return to this tab, which will automatically be redirected to the local history archive

4.) Browse through the articles in the archive by subject heading. You should be able to access all of the articles without having to sign back into the database during the session
